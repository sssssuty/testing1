Overview
As a junior art director at Jane Smith Agency, I worked on the digital design for Natural Diamond Council's first-ever global campaign For Moments Like No Other, a digital campaign featuring jewelry pieces for special occasions, and presentations for internal usage and external trade partners, with Art Director Alexandra Hehlen.

Digital Banners and Social Media
Featuring Jewellry Based on Special Occasions
Following our big movements of the first-ever global campaign and special Covid campaign, we planned to feature jewelry pieces with a daily life theme. Narrowed down to three occasions: Birth of a Child, Birthday, and Anniversary, I created the digital banners and social media content across multiple channels.
