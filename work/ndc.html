· Overview ·
As a junior art director at Jane Smith Agency, I worked on the digital design for Natural Diamond Council's first-ever global campaign For Moments Like No Other, a digital campaign featuring jewelry pieces for special occasions, and presentations for internal usage and external trade partners, with Art Director Alexandra Hehlen.

I also helped to design the cookie footer UI and the author page for the client's consumer-facing website, Only Natural Diamond, which you may visit here: https://www.naturaldiamonds.com/

· Campaign: For Moments Like No Other ·
Digital Banners
Using the final campaign still, I paired with a designer and an art director to work on the digital banners for eight advertising channels, including the New York Times, Bustle, and Amazon.

To show off all campaign-related visuals, including final campaign assets, BTS, jewelry pieces, jewelry designers, and the production team who made this campaign possible during this challenging time, I partnered with art director Alexandra Hehlen to create a deck for our client and their trade partners.
