Overview
The primary purpose behind the app design below for Jin · He gallery is to experiment with new ways to reach out to individuals. It extends the traditional techniques, website and emails, to a portable native app and built-in alerts. With the current design, visitors, members, and researchers could better access the various kinds of information provided by the cultural institution.
Design Challenge
Designing an app for cultural institutions is a very new concept. There are no successful previous app design for cultural institutions.

My Role
Designing from scratch with understanding of the mission of cultural instutions, user need for both digital and physical visit, membership, and museum database, I am testing out the formula app for cultural institutions.
Project Goals
  1. Build a mobile app to make exhibitions info, knowledge of art works and artists, events, online ticket purchasing, membership more accessible to visitors.
  2. Allow the users to access the database to expand the roles of the institutions.
  3. Users can search the stop number for individual artwork during their visit, and to learn more about the piece.
