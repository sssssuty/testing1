· Overview ·
The purpose of the app design below for Jin · He gallery is to experiment with new ways to reach out to individual visitors/users. It extends the traditional techniques, website and emails, to a portable native app and built-in alerts. With the current design, visitors, members, and researchers could better access various kinds of information provided by the cultural institution. The current solution is a formula for all cultural institutions.

· Main Functions ·





01. Homepage/On View

The homepage is set to the current on view exhibitions, as exhibitions are the products of cultural institutions. From this page, users can click into detail pages of the exhibition pages, and learn more about the shows and artworks.









02. Plan Your Visit

Plan Your Visit is a page where users can purchase tickets and find out about the museum hours. To emphasize and differenciate the ticket purchasing call-to-action function from other information-driven funcitons, it is placed on the right top corner.

It is important to include the Plan Your Visit page because two necessary steps to visit the museum for visitors and tickets are the huge part of the income for cultural institutions.

Once purchased, users can view them in the Account page and show at the entrance of the gallery.









03. Calendar

The calendar is a vital function to public engagement of events and have the potential to drive sales for memberships. All events are highlighted in black and orange dots, representing free events and members excusive events.

It is user-friendly if users can have the option to add the events to their phones' calendar, or be reminded by push alerts from the APP. All registered events will be listed under Account.









04. Map

Users can see the accurate and detailed interactive floorplan with 3D installation model under Map.

It is an improvement of the current printed generalized map that visitors can get during their actual visits to the Jin·He. Vistors can find the pieces that they are interested in viewing before their visits, and have smooth walk around in the cultural institions. Business wise, a digital map is also more sustainable.









05. Learn

Users can search not only stop number but also artwork, artists, and events while they are in the actual showroom or they want to learn more before and after their visits.

The Learn function also provides an opportunity to link the internal database to the public-facing database, and could be a potential factor of membership sellings.









06. Account

Account is where users can log in their memberships and view their purchased tickets and registered events. For a new user or a user who did not log in as member, it will show the cose and benefits of all membership levels at the bottom.









07. Detail Pages

This is how the layout would be for the detailed information of exhibitions, artists, artworks, and established articles from Jin He.

Users can reach to these pages from Homepage/On View, Calendar, Learn, and Account.
