Overview
I had the chance to work on motion graphic pieces for one exhibition, Artistic License and two events, Summer Campaign and 60th Anniversary, and the redesign of the Individual Giving meeting material package during my internship. My motion design for Artistic License was chosen to be the first ever ads for Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum to put on the NYC ferry. I was reported to Graphic Designer Michael Enten and the Director of Graphic Design Jae-eun Chung.
