· Overview ·
I had the chance to work on motion graphic pieces for one exhibition, Artistic License and two events, Summer Campaign and 60th Anniversary, and the redesign of the Individual Giving meeting material package during my internship. My motion design for Artistic License was chosen to be the first ever ads for Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum to put on the NYC ferry. I was reported to Graphic Designer Michael Enten and the Director of Graphic Design Jae-eun Chung.

· Print Material ·
Redesigning the Meeting Material Package for Individual Giving

proposal #1 ↓
I designed this saddle-stitched meeting material package entirely in Microsoft Word. Each information section is placed on one full bleed to make it easier to read. If there's an empty right page, it is an excellent opportunity to showcase Guggenheim's iconic building, interior, or artworks invested by the council.



proposal #2 ↓
The other concept I drafted is more straightforward. With the new council headers, meeting materials are printed on single page paper and gathered in the Guggenheim folder.
