· Overview ·
The first part of this project is the Natural Diamond Council's first-ever global campaign For Moments Like No Other. It was created by the Jane Smith Agency team, where I was the Junior Art Director during the post-production period. I helped created digital banners, emails, campaign decks, and social media content.

Outside of the campaign, Jane Smith Agency also designed a new website for Natural Diamond Council as part of its new brand identity. I designed the Cookie Footer and options for the Author Detail Page.
