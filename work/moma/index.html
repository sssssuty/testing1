· Overview ·
As the Digital Design Manager at MoMA Retail department, known as the MoMA Design Store, I oversee all the digital design, including eNewsletter, website, and social media.
· eNewsletter ·
Optimize and Enhance Performance through Visual Updates
ENewsletters are the closest design to our customers as they are sent directly to the subscribers inbox. We want to seek for opportunity to grow – both the number of subscribers and the click through rate. The question for me is, how to achieve this with visual?
One of the go-to strategy is to create appealing visuals through colors. With large blocks of colors, I was able to build campaign-based creative and convey the sense of urgency during promotions.
The advance approach comes down to content strategy. Collaborated with our Retention Marketing Manager and Site Merchandising Manager, we run tests on how our audience react to different design layouts and image direction.
