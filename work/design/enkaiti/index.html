· Overview ·
Hua Wen Kai Ti is the offical typeface used for all textbook in Mainland China. However, there is no perfect pair of typeface for Pinyin, which is the pronunciation systems presented in Latin language with accents. To solve the problem and furtder explore, I designed a set of English typeface to pair with Hua Wen Kai Ti.

En KaiTi is designed to match the stroke and terminal styles of HuaWenKaiTi with accurate usage of pinyin and punctuation for both language systems.
