Overview
I joined Parisa Wang as a digital & graphic designer in June 2020. Reported directly to the creative director and founder, Parisa Wang, I oversee the digital design for both the US market and the Chinese market. Since June, I have rebranded the email and ads system, refreshed the website content with the latest campaign visuals, and expanded the brand existence on WeChat and Xiaohongshu.

My first task is emails. After researching how previous emails were designed and their performances, I constructed a fresh system to showcase the product better and communicate the brand's DNA.

To expand and acquire new customers, we launched our public store on Xiaohongshu. Working as a business account on Instagram, but with the official website features, we emphasize our signature product and lastest campaign to create awareness.




Chinese Market
