Research
User Research Sessions

Initiated by the founding team's personal struggle to prepare for interviews, Enterview AI was born to help students and college graduates to better prepare for interviews.

The team conducted 50+ user interviews sessions to understand their pain points and needs.

Common pain points include:
#1 Lack of reliable methods to assess prepared answers.
While friends can be helpful during the preparation process, they often lack expertise in the specific field, making it difficult for them to provide accurate feedback on the quality of your answers.

#2 Uncertainty about which questions to prioritize for preparation.
Although there are numerous posts and articles online, it’s challenging to determine which questions are most relevant and likely to be asked in their specific interview.

#3 Difficulty in creating realistic mock interview sessions.
Interviews often involve follow-up questions or interruptions, making it hard to simulate this experience with people who lack relevant experience.



Solution
Enterview.ai is a platform designed to help users identify relevant interview questions based on the job description, craft high-quality, personalized answers, track practice progress, receive feedback, and organize thoughts through notes.

Looking ahead, the team plans to expand the service to cover technical interviews, resume preparation, and community building, supporting users throughout the entire job hunting process.
