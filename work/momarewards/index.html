· Role ·
As the Digital Design Manager at The Museum of Modern Art's retail department, MoMA Design Store, I led the digital user experience design and co-led the branding section of this project. I collaborated with stakeholders of multiple department to build this program from 0 to 1.
Problem & Challenge
In 2021 and 2022, the customer return rate for MoMA Design Store is lower than industry average, 16.5%. Thus the team decided to launch a new loyalty program to enhance the customer return rate and viscosity.

The biggest challenge of this project was the timeline. We started the project in February 2022 and completed it by September 2022.



· Website UIUX ·
Website User Flow
I mapped out the core digital experience.Initially, I planned to build a new loyalty program landing page for new customers and logged-out customers to learn about the program and add customers’ loyalty program status to the existing customer profile page.

However, this user journey requires more development work than the team’s bandwidth. To simplify yet refine, I came up with the solution to combine the logged-out landing page and the logged-in landing page so that only a few content modules will be dynamic and keep the original customer profile page.

· Website UIUX ·
Homepage Banners
We dynamically feature the program on the evergreen global nav skinny banner, homepage hero, and a banner at the bottom of the homepage (after we switch the homepage hero to another theme). New and returning customers see different copy versions that are more relevant to them.



Other Key Banners
We also want to surface the program on webpages with a different hierarchy—for example, the product grid and detail pages.



· Website UIUX ·
Program Landing Page – Logged Out
For the logged-out version of the landing page, we want to dive into how the program works and the benefits our customers will enjoy once they sign up for the program. I designed the hero, How the Program Works, Benefit and FAQs modules. We also want to layer in the Museum of Modern Art membership, as the museum members enjoy more benefits.

At the bottom of this page I added a product carousel. If a customer needs to think about it before joining the program, looking at our products will make them want to participate.





Program Landing Page – Logged In
For the logged-in version of the landing page, it is important to highlight the customers’ status and the Ways to Earn module at the top of the page, to motivate our customers to engage.



· Online & Stores ·
How to Combine the In-store and Online Experience?
Unlike shopping online, where points can be automatically redeemed during checkout, customers who shop in stores must show the redemption code at checkout. Ideally, I would create customized landing pages for each store’s signage. This way, I can introduce the loyalty program while adding content about individual stores. (MoMA Design Store has three physical stores in New York City. Each store has a slightly different product assortment and store events.)

However, given the tight timeline and bandwidth, I must combine the store experience with the online experience.



· Visual Design ·
Brand Identity
Led by the Art Director, the creative team explored two visual directions. The first direction highlights MoMA Design Store’s core mission: “to bring good design into people’s lives by putting it into their homes and hands.” The other concept conveys the idea of a loyalty program visually. After two rounds of review with the marketing team, the second option was chosen to revise and refine.




· Visual Design ·
Email Campaigns
Our art director and graphic designer designed all the promotional emails, from program announcement to welcome email, from point reminder notification to birthday bonus. I helped with building all emails into HTML files and the dynamic modules with an external partner.

· Visual Design ·
Social Media Content
We want to promote this program on social media as well. I designed all social media content and collaborated with the Assitant Director of Branding and External Events.
