· Overview ·
The "Team MoMA" campaign was initiated to celebrate and to better visually present the collaboration between MoMA and Champion.

Led by Art Director Amy Kim and Producer & Stylist Lisa Carr, the below photo direction was selected within three proposed concepts, which expanded to logo & identity, pop-up space, digital and signage design. All images shot by Leif Huron, Lead Creative Producer, Photography and Image Production.


· Art Direction ·
Before we landed on the name or the final art direction concept, each of our team member contributed one photo direction.



After three rounds of review and touch bases internally (creative team), and with the buyers, marketing team, and the Museum side, we landed on this "Team MoMA" concept.

This concept captures the playfulness and team-vibe of the hoodie, and add visual narrative to the products.








· Website ·
Using the final still, we created different website visual asset to "show off" and celebrate the campaign.



· eNewsletter ·
We had a Member Early Access enews, a full-file enews and several subfeature modules to advocate the campaign, products, and the pop-up space.





· Social Media ·
Our strategy for this campaign is to tease the campaign two weeks before its official launch. We wanted to create excitement around the product on social media.

We launched the hoodies as the official launch but the caps and beanies came after as the second and third batches of the campaign, aiming to continue the hype and expanding the scope of the campaign.






· Pop-up Space ·
We built a pop-up space at our soho store so that people can come and feel the campaign & products.
