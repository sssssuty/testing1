Overview
Working as a Junior Art Director at Jane Smith Agency, I had the opportunity to take the initiative in designing a brand new site for the agency. I took a leadership role in the initial research and user flow planning and collaborated with a senior art director, a web designer, and copywriters, to finish the design and asset upload.

https://www.janesmithagency.com/
Initial Research
Aiming to build a website that can showcase the agency's personality, specialties and past projects,
I researched how other agencies have their site designed strategically.




User Flow
I landed on the critical web page with my initiative research, including Home, Work, About, Service, and Contact,
to make the site straightforward and concise after my initial research.
