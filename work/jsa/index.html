· Overview ·
Working as a Junior Art Director at Jane Smith Agency, I had the opportunity to design a brand new site for the agency.

I took a leadership role in the initial research and building the information architecture. I communicated directly with a programmer and adjusted the design to fit best for both the visual presentation and the backend. I collaborated with a senior art director, a web designer, and copywriters to finish uploading all assets. I also researched and initiated the SEO strategy for each page and critical assets.

https://www.janesmithagency.com/
· Initial Research ·
Aiming to build a website that can showcase not only the past projects but only the agency's personality and specialties, I researched how other agencies have their information architecture planned out strategically.




· User Flow ·
I provided serveral flows to my team, and we landed on these critical web pages below, including Home, Work, About, Service, and Contact, to make the site straightforward and concise after my initial research.

· Visual Design ·
Because the agency provides a wide range of service, from branding, marketing strategy and pricing, to campaign, digital design, social media, and websites, I collaborated with a web designer and a senior art director to test out the visual design of key elements to find the best way to tell our story.


Landing Page Opening Explorations ↓
We had several iterations of landing page opening section that highlights a concise quote that shows the personality and the agency reel.



Case Study Thumbnail Explorations ↓
I introduced two ways of presenting each project on the landing page, indicating the scale of each case study. The team decided to use the larger one for all case studies because the full bleed background is more impactful and clean.



Case Study Detail Page Opening Explorations ↓
I explored different ways to tell the story in the beginning of each case study page.



Content Block Explorations ↓
Because the agency provides a wide range of services, I introducd different content blocks that would showcase each kinds of work better. I also collaborated with programmers to test out the correct size and creative way to solve sizing issues.
