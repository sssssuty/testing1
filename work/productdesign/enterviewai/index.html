Overview
Enterview AI is an innovative interview preparation platform that uses AI to help job seekers craft well-structured, personalized answers for behavioral interview questions.
Objective
Identify key user pain points around interview preparation and engagement.
Create a more intuitive user journey through targeted research and UX optimization.
Leverage AI technology to deliver tailored feedback that improves user confidence and interview outcomes.
Strategic Value
Through collaborative research, we identified the reasons behind low user engagement and structured answers. The redesign strategy focused on enhancing the user experience, integrating personalized AI features, and improving communication of Enterview AI’s unique value proposition.

This strategy aimed to build user confidence, increase the platform’s value perception, and foster long-term engagement.

Challenges
As Enterview AI prepared to launch, we identified several key challenges that needed to be addressed to narrow the product’s focus and ensure a successful market introduction.
01 Broad product scope Initially, the product aimed to address multiple aspects of interview preparation, which diluted its impact. Users struggled to see the specific value in behavioral interview prep amidst a sea of features.
02 Uncertainty about AI value A key concern was whether the AI model would deliver real value to users. While the AI had the potential to analyze user responses and provide personalized feedback, there was uncertainty around how accurate, relevant, and actionable this feedback would be in real-world scenarios.
03 Clarifying the core feature With the wide-ranging capabilities of AI, it was important to zero in on the one feature that would resonate most with users: offering structured, personalized responses to behavioral interview questions, which would help users stand out in their interviews.
How might we ensure that Enterview AI’s personalized, AI-driven behavioral interview preparation delivers real value to users and supports them in achieving success in their job interviews?
Research
To refine Enterview AI’s focus and ensure the AI model delivered real value, my team and I conducted extensive research, including user interviews, feedback sessions, and competitive analysis.
Interviewed 10 job seekers across various industries (e.g., tech, finance, and healthcare) to understand their interview prep struggles and expectations from an AI-powered tool.
Collected feedback from 5 career coaches to evaluate how well Enterview AI could provide structured, actionable insights similar to human feedback.
Performed a competitive analysis of existing interview preparation tools, identifying gaps in personalized, real-time feedback that Enterview AI could address.
This research helped us pinpoint key user pain points and validate the AI model’s potential to provide personalized support. Using insights from competitor benchmarking and expert feedback, I led my team in narrowing the product’s scope to focus on AI-powered behavioral interview preparation, ensuring the tool offered practical and relevant feedback to improve user outcomes.
