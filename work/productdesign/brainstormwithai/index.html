Overview
Among all the Enterview AI features, Brainstorm with AI is its core conversational feature that guides users through a conversation, asking questions and providing feedback to help users structure their answers effectively. I joined the team after the launch of its MVP, and my primary focus was to refine the feature based on user feedback and testing sessions.
Objectives
Conduct contextual inquiry and user interviews to understand how users use the MVP feature.
Redesign the feature base on research findings and insights.
Validate the redesign through user testing sessions.
Outcome

Reflection
01. Balancing Human-Like Qualities in Conversational AI Design
When designing conversational AI, it is essential to compare AI-generated responses with those of humans, as our users are human and are familiar with how others typically react to messages. While we aim for the AI to exhibit intelligence and human-like qualities, it is crucial to clarify that the goal is not to create an AI that impersonates a human. This nuanced approach is vital to maintain transparency and authenticity in user interactions.
02. Collaboration for Effective AI Response Tonality
The tone of AI responses should be an essential part of the design goals, requiring teamwork between the tech and design teams. This collaboration is especially important because describing the ideal tone/copywriting can be difficult.
