Tingyu Su
Experience Project
Ms.Who
*Print
2018
Ms.Who magazine conveys the idea "you are special," to educate viewers to explore and discover their own fashion styles, instead of following others'. Designed to face Chinese college students currently studying in the United States who are between 18 to 25 years old, Ms. Who includes affordable fashion products to the potential audiences and designer fashion brands which are not well known but do have interesting fashion tastes.
Backing up my idea, I wrote about Tina Chow and Shirley Chisholm, interviewed Aime Lufan Chen, who grew up in Beijing, China and currently studying in SVA, and Yingchun Xi, a fashion lover born in the 60s.
