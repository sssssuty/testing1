Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum Meeting Material Template

2019
One of the assignments for me as an intern at the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum is to redesign the meeting material template for Individual Giving under the Development Department. The challenge is to figure out a text-based system that fits the general direction of the rebrand of the institution and to design everything in Word document.

I presented two ideas in the first round of review. The first direction below allows all material to be printed on tabloid paper and bound with a saddle stitch into a pamphlet. My colleagues from Development chose the second direction below in the end because it involves less human labor.
