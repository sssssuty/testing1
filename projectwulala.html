Wulala
*Digital Product
*Programming
2020
Wulala is a game-like learning webpage for kids, aiming to let kids learn about cities with the function of doodling. Once a valid city name is entered, a color option will be generated based on the current temperature of that entered city, and a stroke will be added based on the length of the city name. Erase and save functions are built too.
