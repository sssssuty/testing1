NAD Search Edu
· Website UIUX
2019
Initially, I was only hoping to build a digital product to serve the hard of hearing and the deaf community. I drafted several potential problems that I can build a digital product around and landed on Search Edu after rounds of interviews. 5 of 9 hard of hearing people who I have talked to have experienced hardship towards finding suitable education. 6 of 6 people who have helped me with sign language translation had heard complaints about education from their hard of hearing friends.
Problem
· No public platform for hard of hearing community to do research on eduction.
· Not easy for the community to connect with people from the community online.
· Not trustworthy source to rate instituions based on the needs of the community.
Solution
· Include exclusive information gathered for the community.
· Build a community function to ask and answer.
· Balance the logo type and secondary type.
· Add a badge system designed to rate all colleges in the United States towards the needs of the community.
