To make the film industry less distant, TV channels proxi is build to help young adults and teenagers, who want to pursue film and related path as their career, to learn about the film industry. Shows and schedules were carefully designed to show all steps and roles behind films, from producers and directors to actors and actresses, from an editor, composer to computer effect designer and all!
