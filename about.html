Tingyu Su
·About· Work
Believing in the power of design, Tingyu Su is a multidisciplinary designer who contructs creative projects crossing different platforms and mediums. She values ideation, process and final execution. She is also a lover of system and culture.

With a BFA in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design, she has previously interned at the Rubin Museum of Art, Jane Smith Agency, Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum, and studio Man-84, and collaborated with art directors, social media strategiests, and musicians on self-initiated projects.
