LACMA Mobile APP Redesign
*Digital Product
2019
Interested in digital products for cultural institutions, I found the Los Angeles County Museum of Art(LACMA) Mobile APP. Mobile APP could be very helpful for all cultural institutions. It is helpful for user/visitors to tour around the institution, to do research on collections, and to manage membership. For institution, a mobile app is helpful to gather information from the visitors and promote events, tickets and membership.
Problem
·Hard to find functions.
·Icons and type size not consistant.
·Emphasizing the logo type too much.
·No ticket and event functions to get users involved.
Solution
·Rearrange all functions.
·Resize icons and text.
·Balance the logo type and secondary type.
·Add event, map, and ticket funtions.
