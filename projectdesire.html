Info Work
Desire Poster Series
*Motion Graphics
2018
Looking closely into daily desires, or craves, five of them are selected - the desire of caffeine, calories, sleep, phone-ing, and shopping. Starting the animation with typography and images, the posters end with a blessing at the end - from “the desire of calories” to “the desire of being healthy”. This series was showcased in Topics: Motion Graphics class and projected inside of 2 W 13th street building lobby, Parsons School of Design.
