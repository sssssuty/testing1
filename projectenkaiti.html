Hua wen kai ti is the offical typeface used for all textbook in Mainland China. However, there is no perfect pair of typeface for the Pinyin, which is the pronunciation systems presented in Latin language with accents. To solve the problem and further explore, I designed a set of English typeface to pair with Hua wen kai ti.
